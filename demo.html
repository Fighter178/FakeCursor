Fake Cursor Demo (fkcs)

Hilighting is not supported.

Instructions

  • Click on a blank part of the screen
  • Move your (now fake) cursor around
  • To escape, press escape

Advantages

  • User cannot move cursor over restricted areas (v2+)
  • Keep users on page logner
  • Complete cursor control (v2+), eg. moving it with JS/TS
  • Resize cursor on specific pages (use resize function)
